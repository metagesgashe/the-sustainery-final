Today’s clothing production system is extremely wasteful and polluting. In fact, the apparel industry is the #2 most polluting industry in theworld.

Large amounts of non-renewable resources are ulitized to produce textiles and fabric. The post-industrial textile waste ends up in landfills or incinerators.

The fashion industry has a pollution problem and consumers want brands to help them to improve their environmental footprints by offering more sustainable choices.

SOLUTION

The Sustainery is a B2B marketplace offering a solution that supports a circular economy and enables a more effective system for utilizing unwanted,wasted and deadstock textiles. We connect suppliers and sustainable designers through a collaborative effort with unwanted textiles that would otherwise end upin a landfill.

MARKET OPPORTUNITY

16 million tons of textile wast is generated each year. Consumers are increasingly aware of their impact which is driving growth of sustainable fashion companies by small to medium sized companies

There are at least 50 new sustainable fashion brand company in the US that are less than 5 years old advertising the use of deadstock textiles, driving up the demand for access to these resources.

96% of people surveyed believe their own actions make a difference and 88% want more sustainable brands and choices. Opportunities exist through community partnerships. Plans to join industry and waste diversion programs such as ohio by-products synergy network and others with a focus on sustainable solutions.

HOW WE’RE DIFFERENT

  • We provide alternative models for accessing sustainable fabricshow we're different
  • No physical inventory, offering streamed, direct ship model
  • Help turn wast into profit for both suppliers and designer
  • single source for deadstock fabric and textiles
  • The goods ship directly from the supplier to the buyer

BUSINESS MODEL

Offer access to textiles that emerging Designers may not typically have access to due to the large minimum requirements of mills.

Utilize the most Efficient Logistics by shipping directly from supplier to buyer.

OUR COMMITMENT/IMPACT: We connect designers with unique fabrics to support their brand offerings such as sustainable options that allows them to test new fabrics with lower commitment and risk.

OUR COMMITMENT/IMPACT: Textiles and fabrics will not go into landfills

Help Suppliers optimize a system to repurpose waste through networking.

Earn a Commission on each sale with variable mark-ups depending on what the supplier needs to recover or off-set a loss.
